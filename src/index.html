Browser Port Experiments

Real, full browsers running entirely inside your browser — no servers doing the rendering. Each one is a complete operating system emulated in WebAssembly (v86), drawn to a <canvas>, and connected to the live internet over the Wisp protocol (default wss://anura.pro).

Pick a browser below. The first launch streams the OS image on demand, so give it a moment. Click into the screen to capture your keyboard/mouse.

Logic: x86 emulated to WebAssembly · Graphics: canvas · Networking: Wisp → wss://anura.pro. OS images streamed from copy.sh's CDN.
